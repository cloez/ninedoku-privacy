```
Pure logic, pure joy — Ninedoku.

No ads. No internet. Just Sudoku.
Ninedoku is a beautifully designed, fully offline Sudoku app that respects your time and privacy.

◆ YOUR LEVEL, YOUR PACE
From beginner to expert, 6 difficulty levels ensure there's always the right challenge for you.

• Starter — New to Sudoku
• Easy — Know the basics
• Medium — Comfortable with basic techniques
• Hard — Ready for advanced strategies
• Expert — Love a tough challenge
• Master — The ultimate test

◆ MULTIPLE GAME MODES
Beyond classic Sudoku, enjoy variations like Diagonal Sudoku and Block Sudoku. Same rules, fresh twists, endless fun.

◆ DAILY PUZZLES
A new puzzle every day. Build your streak, sharpen your skills, and never run out of challenges.

◆ TRACK YOUR PROGRESS
Detailed statistics show your game time, best records, and clear rates. Watch your improvement unfold through beautiful charts.

◆ COLLECT BADGES
Complete challenges to earn badges. From your first clear to epic streaks, there's always something new to achieve.

◆ FULLY OFFLINE
No internet required — ever. Play on a plane, in the subway, anywhere. The app doesn't even have internet permission, so your data stays completely private.

◆ NO ADS · NO TRACKING
Zero advertisements. No analytics SDKs, no tracking tools, no data collection of any kind. Just pure gameplay.

◆ 8 LANGUAGES
한국어, English, 日本語, 中文, हिन्दी, Español, العربية, Français
```
